# About
Goblinoid.com was created in 2018 by Alexander Sousa to archive Tabletop RPG game content and has since grown to include the work of friends in the TTRPG sphere as well as art, music, and more.

Inquiries, suggestions, as well as any interest in collaboration, commissions, or use of material should be sent to goblinoidemail@gmail.com

The site is primarily maintained by Alexander Sousa and Alexander Silva.
